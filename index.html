ABOUT

I am the lead software engineer for one of Colorado's best mortgage brokerages. Move Mortgage is a startup company that assists clients with purchasing and refinancing their home loans. My role is to build and maintain the following:

  • Client Facing: Animated instructional videos and automated email notifications
  • Internal Tools: Loan progress tracking and automated third party item requests
My approach to building for my team is from the ground up. I originally joined our team as a licensed mortgage loan officer and worked directly with our clients to provide an excellent loan experience. You can see past reviews here (Zillow.com).

As I continued to master my craft, I transitioned from sales to software engineering where my sole focus now is to redesign and improve our clients' experience. Today, all Move Mortgage clients receive tailored content that is automated through the tools my team and I have built. This content includes email status notifications and animated instructional videos.

Our team is hard at work with ambitious goals for the future of Move Mortgage! I am excited for what we will build.
